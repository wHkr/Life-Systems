
# Emergency Fund

---

## PURPOSE:

Protect yourself from unexpected expenses without going into debt.

## CORE IDEA:

Life will break things—your car, your plans, or your income. The emergency fund absorbs that impact.

## WHAT IT COVERS:

> 1. Car repairs
> 2. Medical expenses
> 3. Job loss or reduced income
> 4. Urgent, unplanned costs

## HOW TO BUILD IT:

1. Start small:<br>
    * First goal: $500–$1,000<br>
2. Grow over time:<br>
    * Target: 3–6 months of expenses<br>
3. Keep it separate:<br>
    * Stored in a savings account (not checking)<br>

## WHY THIS WORKS:

* Prevents credit card debt
* Reduces stress during emergencies
* Keeps your system stable

## KEY RULES:

> 1. Only use for true emergencies
> 2. Refill it after using it
> 3. Do not invest this money

## RESULT:

You stay in control even when things go wrong.
