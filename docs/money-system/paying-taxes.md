# Paying Taxes
---
## PURPOSE:

Create a predictable, stress-free way to handle taxes.

## CORE IDEA:

Taxes should never be a surprise. The money is set aside as income is earned, not when the bill arrives.

## HOW IT WORKS:
1. Every time income is received:
    - Move ~25–30% into your Tax Account (HYSA)
2. Let the money sit:
    - Earn interest over time
    - Do not touch it
3. When taxes are due:
    - Pay directly from the tax account
### WHY THIS WORKS:
- Removes panic at the end of the year
- Prevents overspending money that isn’t yours
- Earns small returns on money you already owe
### KEY RULES:
> 1. Set aside money immediately<br>
> 2. Never spend from the tax account<br>
> 3. Always assume you owe taxes<br>
## RESULT:

Taxes become routine instead of stressful.
