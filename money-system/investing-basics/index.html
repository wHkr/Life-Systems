    Investing Basics

    PURPOSE:

    Grow money over time instead of just storing it.

    CORE IDEA:

    Money that sits still loses value. Money that is invested has the potential to grow.

    HOW IT WORKS:

    1. Set aside a portion of income:
      • After spending, saving, and taxes
    2. Invest consistently:
      • Regular contributions over time
    3. Let time do the work:
      • Growth compounds year after year

    TYPES OF ACCOUNTS:

    1. Roth IRA

      • Tax-free growth
      • Long-term use

    2. Brokerage Account

      • Flexible access
      • No age restrictions

    KEY CONCEPT: COMPOUNDING

    Money earns → then earns on what it earned

    WHY THIS WORKS:

    • Builds long-term wealth
    • Outpaces inflation
    • Reduces reliance on income alone

    KEY RULES:

    1. Invest for the long term
    2. Do not panic during market drops
    3. Stay consistent over time

    RESULT:

    Your money works for you instead of you working for it forever.
