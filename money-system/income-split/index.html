    4-Account Money System

    FRAMEWORK SECTIONS:

    Breakdown by part:

     Income -> Split (
     1. Spend
     2. Save
     3. Invest
     4. Taxes
 )

    Example:

    $1000
    -> $300 -> Tax account
    -> $500 -> Spending account
    -> $200 -> Savings/Investing accounts

    4-ACCOUNT MONEY SYSTEM BREAKDOWN:

    1. Spending Account (Checking)

    This is your MAIN ACCOUNT
    1. Pay bills
    2. Buy food
    3. Daily life

    2. Savings Account (Emergency Fund)

    This is your SAFETY NET
    1. Car repairs
    2. Unexpected expenses

    3. Investing Account

    This is your LONG-TERM MONEY
    1. Roth IRA
    2. Brokerage

    4. Taxing Account (High-Yield Savings)

    This is your DON'T TOUCH ACCOUNT
    1. Set aside ~25-30% of income
    2. Earn interest
    3. Used ONLY for taxes
